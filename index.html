who is Snoopy?

Snoopy is a fictional dog that has become a popular charecter of the Charlie Brown Cartoons. He is known for having wild imagination as a book lover and writer that will always help his round-headed friend

Snoopy's Alter Ego

Snoopy has a lot of famous alter egos throughout the peanuts cartoons, his most famous being the Flying Ace and Joe Cool. The Flying Ace is a fantasy identity where snoopy takes the role as a WWI Flying Ace as he constatly goes against his arch nemesis the Red Baron. Joe Cool is Snoopy as college student who thinks he is really cool, somewhat like James Dean or Fonzie from Happy Days
